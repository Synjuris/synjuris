SynJuris helps you build your case file, understand relevant legal patterns, and prepare documents — so you walk into court or a lawyer's office ready.

Structured Evidence Organization Log, categorize, and confirm exhibits with auto-assigned numbers and tamper-evident cryptographic sealing.
Pattern Recognition for Research Flags potentially relevant legal categories and related constitutional provisions — as a research starting point, not legal advice.
Cryptographic Evidence Chain Each confirmed exhibit is sealed into a Merkle DAG. Generates a verifiable proof record showing your evidence was not altered post-entry.
AI-Assisted Document Drafting Generates draft documents from your organized evidence. All drafts require your review and attorney verification before filing.

Not legal advice. SynJuris is an organizational and drafting tool, not a law firm. Nothing here constitutes legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney before filing any document or making legal decisions.
