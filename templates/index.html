What is SynJuris?

SynJuris is a local-first legal intelligence environment. It is a private, offline workstation designed to help you map, secure, and win high-stakes litigation without the risks of cloud-based transmission.

By utilizing deterministic logic engines, we provide a mathematical visualization of your case standing, stripping away the emotional static that clouds strategic decision-making.

01 / Case Mapping

Quantify procedural weight across three axes of standing to visualize your position before you file.

02 / Grey Rock Guardrails

Integrated filters strip high-conflict markers, ensuring every draft is court-ready and neutral.

03 / Merkle Integrity

Every exhibit is hashed into a Merkle DAG Ledger, guaranteeing cryptographic evidence integrity.
